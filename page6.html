Science Fiction

Science fiction (sci-fi) is a genre of movies or stories that focuses on advanced science, technology, space, future worlds, or imaginary scientific ideas. These stories
explore “what if” situations—like time travel, robots, aliens, or life in the future—and show how science can affect human life.
